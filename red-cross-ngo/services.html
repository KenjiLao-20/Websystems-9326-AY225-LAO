Frequently Asked Questions

You can donate blood at any Red Cross blood center. Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended. Requirements: 16-65 years old, at least 50kg weight, in good health condition.

Call our 24/7 emergency hotline at 143. Stay calm, follow instructions from emergency responders, and if trained, provide basic first aid while waiting for help.

We offer regular first aid training sessions. Check our Events page for schedules or contact your local Red Cross chapter for training programs.
