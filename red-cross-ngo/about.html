About Philippine Red Cross

75+ Years of Humanitarian Service to the Filipino People

Our Mission

To provide timely, effective, and compassionate humanitarian assistance to the most vulnerable, guided by the Fundamental Principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement.

Our Vision

A Filipino society where everyone is safe, resilient, and empowered through voluntary service and community-based humanitarian action.

Our Values

Humanity, Impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service, Unity, Universality

Our History

1947

Foundation

Philippine Red Cross was established as an independent humanitarian organization.

1990

Major Expansion

Expanded services to include blood collection, disaster response, and health programs nationwide.

2013

Typhoon Yolanda Response

Largest humanitarian operation, serving millions affected by Super Typhoon Haiyan.

2024

Modernization

Digital transformation and community resilience programs across 100+ chapters.
